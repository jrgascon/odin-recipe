Pizza

Description

Pizza is an iconic Italian dish consisting of a round, flat dough base baked with various toppings. The classic Pizza Margherita (tomato, mozzarella, basil) represents the Italian flag colors and is considered the gold standard. It's a versatile, globally-loved dish with countless regional variations, from thin-crust Neapolitan to deep-dish Chicago style.

Ingredients

  • 500g (4 cups) bread flour or tipo "00" flour
  • 325ml (1⅓ cups) lukewarm water
  • 7g (2¼ tsp) active dry yeast or 15g fresh yeast
  • 10g (2 tsp) fine sea salt
  • 15ml (1 tbsp) olive oil
  • 5g (1 tsp) sugar (helps yeast activate)

Preparations

  1. Make the Pizza Dough (24 hours ahead for best results)
  2. Prepare Pizza Sauce
  3. Preheat Oven & Equipment
  4. Shape the Pizza
  5. Top the Pizza
  6. Bake the Pizza
  7. Finish and Serve
